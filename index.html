Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about our vehicles and services.

Are all Vehicles inspected? +

Yes, every vehicle in our inventory undergoes a thorough multi-point inspection to ensure it meets our high standards for safety, performance and reliability. Our certified technicians check all major systems, including engine, transmission, brakes, suspension and more, to provide you with confidence in your purchase.

Do you offer financing options? +

Yes, we provide flexible financing solutions tailored to your needs, making it easier to acquire your desired vehicle with manageable payments plans.

Can I sell my vehicle to you? +

We buy used vehicles from individuals and businesses, offering fair market value for your pre-owned car.

Can I test drive a vehicle? +

Yes, we encourage all potential buyers to take a test drive before making a purchase decision. This allows you to experience the vehicle's performance and features firsthand. You can book a private test drive appointment at your convenience through our contact section.

Do you deliver nationwide? +

Yes, we offer secure and reliable nationwide delivery services ensuring your vehicle arrives safely at your location.

Are the cars new or used? +

Our inventory includes both new and pre-owned vehicles, each clearly labeled for transparency.

What documents do you require for the purchase? +

We require a valid driver's license, proof of insurance, and financial documentation to complete the purchase process.

What documents will I receive? +

You will receive all necessary ownership documents, service records(if applicable), and a full vehicle report.
